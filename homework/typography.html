TYPOGRAPHY

BY: ALAN ALBERRO

WHAT IS TYPOGRAPHY

  1. Merriam-Webster: the style, arrangement, or appearance of typeset matter
  2. Jay Valle: “Typography is basically alphabet fashion.”
  3. “Typography is a tool with which designers can create things. We use it to shape content, give language a physical body and enable the flow of understanding.”

WHY IS TYPOGRAPHY IMPORTANT?

  1. Words and text can make us feel or think a certain way
  2. Typography is an art form
  3. Visual personality of font and written words should connect
