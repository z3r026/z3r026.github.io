Purpose

The purpose of Mov Reviews is provide people with simple and quick access to movie ratings by their fellow human beings. By doing this, people can know which movies they will most likely enjoy and which ones they can wait for to come out on DVD or be rentable. One of the key parts of Mov Reviews will also be having a Parent Guide section so that people can know if there are any scenes in the movie they don't deem appropriate for themselves, family, or friends.

Target Audience

Mov Reviews wants to cator to people of all ages and with all different kind of movie interests. A lot of other movie review websites seem to cator more towards critic reviews or are just not that in depth, but Mov Reviews will allow the audience to follow other profiles and people with similar likes. Anyone from someone in their teenage years going to watch a film with friends to parents wanting to have a movie night on a Friday with their family.

Persona

Brahms is a 25 year old young man who is studying psychology in college and is near graduation. He enjoys observing others and learning from his surroundings. He is employed on his college campus in the psych department and enjoys spending time with his friends. His favorite type of movies are thriller/horror and is curious if he and his friends should watch the new film entitled, 'Lights Out'. Brahms has installed on his phone the Mov Reviews app, so while him and his friends are at dinner, he's able to quickly pull up and see if the film would be appropriate for them and if other reviewers have given it good ratings.
